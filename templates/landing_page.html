🔑 How to Spot Phishing Emails:

  • 🔍 Check the sender's email address carefully Phishers often use domains that look similar to real ones (e.g., "security@company-support.com" instead of "security@company.com")
  • ⏰ Be wary of urgency and threats Phrases like "Your account will be locked in 24 hours" or "Immediate action required" are red flags
  • 🔗 Hover over links before clicking Check where a link actually goes before clicking. If it looks suspicious, don't click!
  • 📎 Be cautious with attachments Unexpected file attachments, especially executable files or macros, can be dangerous
  • ✏️ Look for poor grammar and spelling Many phishing emails contain obvious errors that legitimate companies wouldn't make
